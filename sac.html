The New Jersey Institute of Technology Student Activity Council (SAC) is a group of fun-loving and ambitious students who desire to make an impact at NJIT. We do this by planning and hosting engaging experiences throughout the semester, aimed at bringing the campus community together.

I am one of the Event Coordinators for the Student Activities Council. I use my event planning, budgeting, volunteer mangement, communication, and teamwork skills to host successful events. I have contacted agencies for different acts and supplies to help run 500+ person, school-wide events. These events include Do It Yourself (DIY) Days, Game Shows, Movie Nights, and more!

I have also attended professional conferences including the National Association for Campus Activities (NACA) in Hartford where I was able to network with other student activities councils, meet with vendors, and get ideas on more events to bring to NJIT.
