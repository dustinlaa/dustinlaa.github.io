Projects

Marshmallow Toast Simulator

Created during the HackNJIT 2022 Hackathon by Adrianna Rust, Andrew Dickman, Dustin La, and Hrishikesh Sakunala

-- Best Camping Themed Hack Winner --

Used Python with the Pygame library to create a game where a user toasts their marshmallow to a scrumptious golden brown. Choose a duration of short, medium, or long to toast your marshmallow over the campfire. Based on this duration, you will toast your marshmallow and get it closer to Golden Brown! But be careful, too much toasting will burn your marshmallow. Continue to toast golden brown marshmallows to increase your streak and go for a high score!

Skills Learned: Python, Pygame

Simple Arcade

Simple Arcade is a project I made in my IT-202: Internet Applications Class.

Users play a simple HTML game for points and can register accounts to save their scores. Users can create public and private profiles as well as competitions to challenge other users. There is also a weekly, monthly, and lifetime scoreboard for the game. For users with admin privileges, they can create roles, edit competitions, and assign other users as admins.

Skills Learned: HTML, PHP, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Bootstrap

FIRST Robotics

For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology, FIRST, is an organization promoting STEM and robotics to students in elementary, middle, and high school levels. I had the opportunity to be on a FIRST Robotics Competition Team in my high school years. During this competiton, teams are given a game each year and the challenge of creating a robot to compete in competitions all around the world. FIRST greatly encourages teamwork, problem solving, learning, and leadership.

I was on Team 4361: Roxbotix from Roxbury, NJ. I was on the team for all four years of high school and participated in the electrical and mechanical aspects of the robots. I also volunteered in community events such as demonstrations, mentoring the middle school team, community clean ups, and more.

I became the Chairman's Award Leader during my senior year where I oversaw the production of a video, essay, and presentation to compete for this award. This award showcased how my team is creating leaders and transforming the culture in ways that will inspire greater levels in STEM.

FIRST Robotics has helped me gain a higher interest in STEM and has made a big impact in my life over the 4 years I was competing in high school.
