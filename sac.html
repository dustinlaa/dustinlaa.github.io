The New Jersey Institute of Technology Student Activity Council (SAC) is a group of fun-loving and ambitious students who desire to make an impact at NJIT. We do this by planning and hosting engaging experiences throughout the semester, aimed at bringing the campus community together.
I am one of the Event Coordinators for the Student Activities Council. I have contacted agencies for different acts and supplies to help run 500+ person, school-wide events. These events include Do It Yourself (DIY) Days, Game Shows, Game Nights, and more!
